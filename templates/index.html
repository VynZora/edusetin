Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers About Studying Abroad with Edusetin

Edusetin currently offers study opportunities in Italy, Germany, Singapore, and Kyrgyzstan. From MBBS programs to bachelor’s and master’s degrees, we guide you in selecting the best-fit country and university based on your career goals.

We provide complete end-to-end support, including counseling, university selection, admission process, documentation, visa application assistance, and pre-departure guidance. Our goal is to make your study abroad journey smooth and stress-free.

Many programs, especially in Italy and Germany, are taught in English. However, learning the local language is recommended for daily life and part-time job opportunities. Edusetin provides guidance and resources for language preparation as well.

Costs vary depending on the country and program. For example, MBBS in Kyrgyzstan is affordable compared to Western Europe, while Italy and Germany often provide low tuition fees and scholarships. Our team will give you a detailed financial breakdown during counseling.

Simply reach out to us through our website, Instagram page, or YouTube channel. Book a counseling session, share your academic background, and we’ll help you choose the right program and start your application process step by step.
